About

Due Process League is a community competitive league for the attack-and-defense game Due Process. We are currently in our second season of gameplay, after DFC took the crown of the first!

We primarily operate out of our Discord. We try to keep the website up-to-date, but sometimes things fall through -- you can find the most recent updates there.
